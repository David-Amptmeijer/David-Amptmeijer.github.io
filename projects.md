# Projects

## Mercury Bioaccumulation Modeling
Development and evaluation of mechanistic Hg/MeHg bioaccumulation modules for aquatic ecosystems, including benthic–pelagic coupling and feeding-strategy‑based uptake.

## Marine Hg Speciation (MERCY v2.0)
Contribution to the first full 3‑D biogeochemical mercury model linking atmospheric inputs to fish MeHg concentrations.

## Biological Controls on MeHg
Modelling DOM uptake, demethylation rates, and sponge/benthos pathways in the Mediterranean Sea.

## PCA of Lake Drivers
Investigation of morphological and watershed controls on predicted MeHg response patterns in lakes.
