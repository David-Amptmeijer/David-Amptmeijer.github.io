## Research Profile
Marine scientist working at the interface of **biogeochemistry, ecosystem modelling, and environmental pollution**.  
My research focuses on **mercury (Hg) cycling and methylmercury (MeHg) bioaccumulation in marine food webs**, combining **numerical ecosystem models, field observations, and data analysis** to understand how pollutants move from environmental sources to seafood.

---

## Research Interests
- Mercury (Hg) biogeochemical cycling  
- Methylmercury (MeHg) bioaccumulation in marine food webs  
- Benthos and benthic–pelagic coupling  
- Marine ecosystem and biogeochemical modelling  
- Anthropogenic pollutants and exposure pathways  
- Climate and ecosystem controls on contaminant transfer
